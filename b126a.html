Email a Product Page by following these easy steps:
  1. You have the option to send multiple items at once by adding a single item into each Additional Item text box.
  2. You also have the option to assign a price amount to each item. Leaving it blank will send the email with no pricing. Do not precede any entered amount with the dollar($) sign. Example: 12.99, 0, 12.50
  3. *Indicates required field.
  4. Valid email addresses must contain an ampersand (@) and a period (.).
  5. Click the PreView button to proof email before sending.
